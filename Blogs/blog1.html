Netlify and BitDefender

One of the most unnoticed error one could get while using Netlify is that if they already have BitDefender in their pc. If you have BitDefender installed in your pc and have activated the protection.....congrats you cannot access Netlify.......your builds wont get deployed, you cant even login and you wont be even able to reset your password.

To avoid this problem either do not use BitDefender and Netlify together or stop BitDefender protection when using Netlify
